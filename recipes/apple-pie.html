Apple Pie

Description

Classic and irresistible homemade Apple Pie recipe that will make your grandma jealous. This is the type of pie everyone wants to make for Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

  • 1 Double-pie crust (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 lbs Granny Smith Apples, thinly sliced (peeled and cored)
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp water (egg wash)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425℉F.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in 3 tbsp flour, simmer for 1 minute, whisking constantly. Whisk in 1/4 cup water, 1 cup sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and continue simmer for 3 minutes, whisking frequently then remove from heat.
  3. Place thinly-sliced apples in a large bowl. Sprinkle with 2 tsp cinnamon and toss to combine. Pour sauce over apples and stir to coat the apple slices.
  4. Sprinkle work surface with flour and roll out pie crust to 12" diameter. Transfer over to pie 9" pie plate. Add apple mixture starting from the center.
  5. Roll second crust into an 11" diameter and cut into 10 thick strips. Arrange strips in a woven lattice pattern over the top. Beat together 1 egg and 1 tbsp water and brush top with egg wash.
  6. Bake at 425℉F in the center of oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat ot 350℉F and continue baking for another 45 minutes or until the filling is bubbling through the vents. Take out and let the pie rest at room temp for 1 hour before serving.
