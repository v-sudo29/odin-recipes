Honey Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Chewy, sweet deliciousness! These treats are a family favorite in every household.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup honey
  • 2 large eggs, room temp, lightly beaten
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350℉F. Wet ingredients: In a bowl, mix shortening, peanut butter, and honey. Add eggs, mix well. Dry ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in 3 tbsp flour, simmer for 1 minute, whisking constantly. Whisk in 1/4 cup water, 1 cup sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and continue simmer for 3 minutes, whisking frequently then remove from heat. Add to peanut butter mixture and mix well.
  3. Roll into 12 2-inch balls and place on baking sheets. Flatten with fork dipped in flour. Bake 8-10 minutes, until set. Remove from oven to cool.
