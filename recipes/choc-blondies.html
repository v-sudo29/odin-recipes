Chocolate Chip Blondies

Description

If you're a fan of classic chocolate chip cookies, these bars will fulfill that craving. Perfect for any occasion with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter melted
  • 2 large eggs, room temp, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350℉F. Wet ingredients: In a bowl, combine brown sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla. Dry ingredients: Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Spread onto greased baking pan. Bake 10-15 minutes, toothpick test must come out clean from center. Take out and cool. Cut into bars.
